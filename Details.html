Open Water Diver

Your Adventure Into the Depths Begins Here

Embark on the journey of a lifetime with our PADI Open Water Diver course. This is the world's most popular and widely recognized scuba course. Millions of people have learned to scuba dive and gone on to discover the wonders of the aquatic world through this course. You'll learn the fundamentals of scuba diving, including dive equipment and techniques, and earn a certification that is recognized worldwide.

Our experienced instructors will guide you through a combination of knowledge development, confined water dives to practice skills, and open water dives to put your skills to the test and explore. By the end of the course, you'll be a confident and certified diver, ready to explore the underwater world on your own.

Prerequisites

  • Minimum age: 10 years old.
  • Adequate swimming skills and ability to float/tread water for 10 minutes.
  • Good physical health (medical questionnaire required).
  • No prior scuba diving experience necessary.
